Practical analytics, measurement, and automation services for app and growth teams — built to produce **clear decisions, reliable tracking, and repeatable performance improvements**.

**For:** app studios, subscription apps, gaming  
**Outcome:** trustworthy conversion tracking + attribution you can act on

**Typical deliverables**
- End-to-end measurement map (events → conversions → revenue)
- Tracking audit + fixes (gaps, inconsistencies, missing revenue signals)
